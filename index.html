"Hello! Snooker World" World Snooker Championship 2022

The World Snooker Championship 2022 was organised by the World Snooker Tour and sponsored by sports betting company Betfred. It featured a total prize fund of £2,395,000, of which the winner received £500,000. The main stage of the tournament featured 32 players: the top 16 players from the snooker world rankings and an additional 16 players from the qualifying rounds.

2022.04.16(Sat) ~ 2022.05.02(Mon)

@ Crucible Theatre in Sheffield, England
